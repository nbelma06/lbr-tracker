Running progression
Earning the run instead of forcing it — this is built around your left IT band, not in spite of it.
Weeks 1–4 · Base building
Incline treadmill walking and StairMaster only, as programmed in your sessions. Builds cardio capacity and hip stabilizer strength without repetitive impact.
Weeks 5–6 · Walk/run intro
4 min walk / 1 min easy jog, repeated for 20 min, flat ground or 0–1% incline only. Only start this once weeks 1–4 are done with zero sharp pain.
Weeks 7–8 · Building volume
If intervals felt clean, shift to 3 min walk / 2 min jog. Any sharp pinch in the left hip/IT band means dropping back a week — not pushing through.
After week 8
Extend jog intervals gradually, no more than ~10% volume increase per week. Continuous outdoor running becomes realistic from here.
